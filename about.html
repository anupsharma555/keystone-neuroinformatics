About

Keystone Informatics is dedicated to advancing the use of data, technology, and analytical methods in healthcare, life sciences, and research settings. We work with organizations to improve data infrastructure, enable evidence-based decision making, and develop solutions that support better outcomes.

Our approach combines expertise in informatics, analytics, and digital health with a commitment to rigorous methodology and practical implementation. We support projects across the healthcare continuum, from basic research to clinical care, with a focus on creating sustainable, effective solutions.
