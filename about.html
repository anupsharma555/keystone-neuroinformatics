About Us

Keystone Neuroinformatics combines expertise in neuroscience and computational methods to support research and development in the field of behavioral health and psychiatry.

Our Mission

We aim to advance neuroscience research by developing and applying computational tools and analytical methods to complex behavioral health data.

Our Approach

We leverage modern data science techniques, machine learning, and computational neuroscience methods to extract meaningful insights from psychiatric datasets.

Core Values

  • Scientific rigor and reproducibility
  • Open collaboration and knowledge sharing
  • Innovation in computational methods
  • Commitment to advancing neuroscience research
